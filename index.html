Established in 2008, Taj Restrow has been a cornerstone of culinary excellence in Jamshedpur. Our restaurant combines the rich heritage of traditional Indian cuisine with modern gastronomy, creating an unforgettable dining experience.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We maintain the highest standards of hygiene with our ISO 22000:2018 certified kitchen. Every dish is crafted using premium ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers, ensuring both taste and safety.

At Taj Restrow, we believe in creating not just meals, but memories. Our expert chefs, led by Master Chef Rajesh Kumar, bring over two decades of culinary expertise to your plate. From aromatic biryanis to sizzling tandoor specialties, each dish tells a story of passion and perfection.
